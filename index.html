About Us

INORA is a perfume brand that blends timeless elegance with global influence. Inspired by Kolkata, each fragrance combines notes like Mediterranean citrus, Middle Eastern amber, Madagascar vanilla, and Indian jasmine, creating a unique sensory experience. More than just a scent, INORA evokes emotions and memories, offering a subtle yet lasting impression of sophistication and allure.

STORY

MISSION

FOUNDER

INORA began as a shared dream between three friends, united by a passion for fragrance and an admiration for the rich culture of Kolkata. Inspired by the city’s timeless elegance, we wanted to create something that transcended the ordinary—a fragrance that told a story, evoked emotions, and left a lasting impression.

With this vision in mind, we set out to craft perfumes that weren’t just scents, but experiences. Each bottle of INORA reflects our journey, blending influences from across the world

At INORA, our mission is to craft fragrances that go beyond the ordinary. We aim to create scents that tell stories, evoke emotions, and capture moments in time. Each bottle is a reflection of our commitment to quality, artistry, and luxury. By blending influences from around the world, we offer a unique sensory experience that transcends tradition and embraces the essence of elegance. We strive to empower those who wear INORA to embrace their individuality, leaving a lasting impression that resonates with power, mystique, and allure.
